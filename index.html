About

What do we do?

PoliSEEP is a platform designed for students and university professors. It was mainly designed for STEM disciplines.
There are two users who use our website:

  • PROFESSORS: they use PoliSEEP to quickly provide teaching material and to create quizzes to be submitted to students. All this is possible for every course the professor teaches.
  • STUDENTS: they use PoliSEEP to access the teaching material of all the courses they are enrolled in. In addition, they can train with the quizzes made available by the teachers.

Using HTML(HyperText Markup Language), CSS(Cascading Style Sheet), JavaScript, we can make learning more easier and in a interesting way.
